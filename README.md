## ğŸ’¡ Key Concepts

### The Judaizers' Error
- Faith in Christ **plus** Torah observance for salvation
- Not rejecting Jesus â€” adding to Jesus
- Paul's verdict: "a different gospel" = no gospel

### Why It Matters
- If law saves, Christ died for nothing
- Adding to Christ insults His sufficiency
- Returns people to bondage they cannot bear

### Shadow vs. Substance
| Shadow (Old Covenant) | Substance (Christ) |
|-----------------------|-------------------|
| Sacrifices repeated | One sacrifice, forever |
| Earthly tabernacle | Heavenly reality |
| External ceremonies | Internal transformation |
| Temporary | Eternal |

### From vs. For
- **Wrong:** Obedience **toward** acceptance
- **Right:** Obedience **from** acceptance
